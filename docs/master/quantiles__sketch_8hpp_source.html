479 * Long || Start Byte Addr:
480 * Addr:
481 * || 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 |

484 * || 8 | 9 | 10 | 11 | 12 | 13 | 14 | 15 |

486 *
487 * Long 3 is the start of data, beginning with serialized min and max item, followed by
